About Me

    My primary area of work is in computational statistics, econometrics, and modeling of complex and dynamical systems.

    Lately I have been interested in how biological systems reason and process information. Inspired by the work of Karl Friston, Michael Levin, and others, I am approaching these issues from the perspectives of statistical physics, developmental biology, and the philosophy of consciousness.

    Basically, I have lots of questions, very few (i.e., precisely zero) answers, I have no idea how it all ties together, and yet am beyond fascinated by it all.

    I can be reached at regmsd@gmail.com.
